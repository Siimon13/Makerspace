About MakerSpace

The NYU Tandon School of Engineering MakerSpace is a new, collaborative work space and lab on the Brooklyn campus. It highlights new kinds of iterative, interdisciplinary teamwork using cutting-edge tools of rapid prototyping and digitally driven production. The MakerSpace is located on the ground floor of Rogers Hall.
